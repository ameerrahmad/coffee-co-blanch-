Quick Answers

Frequently Asked

The things everyone wants to know.

What time do you open?

We're open daily from 5PM to 2AM. Come for coffee, stay for the vibe.

Is there a no-stags policy?

Yes. Coffee Co. Blanche is a family and couples friendly space. Stag groups are not permitted — no exceptions.

How do I get event tickets?

Follow us on Facebook and Instagram for event announcements. Tickets are typically RS. 1000 and include a free coffee.

Do you take reservations?

For general visits, just walk in. For events or large group bookings, reach out via the form above or DM us on social.

Where exactly are you located?

We're in Gulberg 3, Lahore. Search "Coffee Co. Blanche" on Google Maps for directions.

Do you have dairy-free options?

Yes — several of our drinks can be made with oat or almond milk. Ask your barista for details.
