About Coffee Co. Blanche

Born in Lahore. Built on community. Brewed with love.

How It All Began

"Desi Nostalgia Meets Late-Night Coffee Energy"

Coffee Co. Blanche was born from a simple idea: Lahore deserved a café that felt like home — warm, a little chaotic, deeply familiar, and alive after midnight. We opened our doors in Gulberg 3 and never looked back.

From post-Iftar pickups to Eid night magic, from live match screenings to parody nights with Offscript — CCB isn't just a café, it's a feeling. The kind where every corner tells a story and every cup is a reason to stay a little longer.

We're open daily from 5PM to 2AM, because great conversations don't happen at noon.
